# Sample ALJ scouting reports (prototype 03)

These are **deterministic renders** of the per-ALJ dossiers — zero LLM in the
loop. Every line traces to a dossier field, so the output is grounded by
construction. Two modes are shown: a recent ALJ with both structured-outcome
data and a 35-year gold footprint (Sarli), and a historical ALJ with a
gold-footprint-only profile (Doyle, 1979–1984).

All citations are **District (ALJ)** only, following the human volumes'
convention; respondent names are de-identified (R1, R2, … are pseudonymous
roster refs, not names). Minor OCR artifacts (e.g. "district1s") are
extraction noise from the image-era volumes.
